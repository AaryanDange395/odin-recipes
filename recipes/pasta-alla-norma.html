Pasta Alla Norma

Description

Pasta alla Norma is a classic Sicilian pasta dish that is made with eggplant, tomatoes, basil, and ricotta salata. The dish is named after the opera “Norma” by Vincenzo Bellini, and it is said that the dish was created in honor of the composer. The recipe requires rigatoni pasta, eggplant, garlic, canned tomatoes, fresh basil, ricotta salata, and olive oil. The preparation time is 15 minutes, and the cooking time is 30 minutes.

Pasta alla Norma is a delicious and hearty vegetarian dish that is perfect for a cozy dinner at home. The combination of tender eggplant, tangy tomato sauce, and salty ricotta salata creates a flavor explosion in your mouth. The dish is easy to make and can be customized to suit your taste preferences. You can add more garlic or spice it up with red pepper flakes. You can also substitute the rigatoni pasta with any other pasta of your choice.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggplants (about 2 pounds)
  • 2 tablespoons kosher salt
  • 1/2 cup olive oil, divided
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 28 ounce can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning blend (or dried oregano)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 pound rigatoni
  • 1/2 cup basil, torn
  • 1/2 cup ricotta salata, crumbled
